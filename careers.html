Careers & Learning Opportunities

By Amar Sawant, we believe in nurturing new talent. We offer learning experiences, apprenticeships, and mentorship programs for artists and enthusiasts looking to grow in the tattoo industry. Whether you’re starting your first tattoo journey or aiming to refine your skills, we provide guidance, hands-on practice, and industry insights.

Apprenticeships & Learning Programs

Apprenticeship Program

Work alongside our experienced artists to learn tattoo techniques, hygiene, and client interaction. Ideal for beginners or aspiring tattoo professionals.

Skill Development Workshops

Short-term workshops focusing on specific skills such as shading, line work, or digital design for tattooing. Perfect for artists wanting to specialize.

Mentorship & Shadowing

Shadow professional tattoo artists during sessions and gain practical knowledge about studio operations, client management, and creative processes.

Industry Insight

The tattoo industry continues to grow, with new studios offering structured learning programs and mentorship opportunities. According to Inked Magazine, apprenticeships are becoming the preferred path for aspiring artists, providing hands-on experience while building a strong portfolio. Learning and mentorship programs can open doors to diverse roles including studio management, design, and creative direction.

Get Started

If you are interested in joining our learning programs or apprenticeships, email us at careers@yourtattoostudio.com with your portfolio or artwork. We welcome passionate learners of all levels.
