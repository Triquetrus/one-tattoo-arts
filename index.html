Post-Session Care

AFTERCARE
GUIDE

Your ink is a living artwork.

How you care for your tattoo in the first weeks determines how it ages over a lifetime. Follow these steps precisely for the sharpest, most vibrant result.

01
Wrap & Protect
Keep the tattoo wrapped with our provided film for 2–4 hours post-session. This protects against bacteria and abrasion during the most vulnerable period.
02
Gentle Cleansing
Wash 2–3 times daily with lukewarm water and unscented soap. Pat dry with a clean paper towel — never rub.
03
Moisturize Daily
Apply a thin layer of fragrance-free moisturizer (we recommend Bepanthen or Hustle Butter) 3–4 times daily until fully healed.
04
Avoid & Protect
No swimming, sauna, or direct sunlight for 3 weeks. Do not pick at peeling skin. After healing, always apply SPF 50+ before sun exposure.
⚕

STERILE ENVIRONMENT

Every workstation is thoroughly sterilized before and after each session. All needles and cartridges are single-use and disposed of immediately. Your health is our non-negotiable priority.

Does getting a tattoo hurt? +
Yes — but in the best way. Pain varies significantly by placement, design complexity, and personal tolerance. Areas with thin skin or bone close to the surface (ribs, spine, feet) are more sensitive. Most clients describe the sensation as a meditative, even addictive scratch. Your body releases endorphins throughout, which many find pleasurable.
How long does the healing process take? +
Surface healing typically completes in 2–3 weeks. Full skin-depth healing takes 3–6 months. During this time, you may notice peeling, itching, and slight color fading — all completely normal. Avoid sun exposure and keep the area moisturized for optimal long-term results.
What does a tattoo at One Tattoo Arts cost? +
All tattoos start at ₹3,000 for small, simple designs. Final pricing depends on size, complexity, placement, color, and your chosen artist. Large or full-day sessions are quoted individually. We offer free in-person or video consultations to provide an accurate, personalized quote with no surprises.
Is a deposit required to book? +
Yes. A 25% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking and initiates the design process. This deposit is applied toward your final session price. Deposits are non-transferable between artists or dates, though a single reschedule with 72 hours notice may be granted.
What is your minimum age requirement? +
You must be 18 years or older. Valid, government-issued photo ID is required at your appointment — without exception. We enforce a strict no-minors policy with zero exceptions, regardless of parental consent.
Can I bring a reference image or custom design? +
Absolutely — and we encourage it. Bring reference images, mood boards, sketches, or ideas. Our artists will interpret and elevate your vision into a bespoke piece. If you have an existing design you'd like tattooed, we'll review it during consultation for any necessary adaptations.
Do you do cover-ups and reworks? +
Yes. Cover-ups and reworks are specialty services we offer selectively based on the existing tattoo and desired result. We evaluate each case individually during a dedicated consultation. Not all tattoos are coverable — honesty about feasibility is something we commit to from the start.
